Boys with DMD typically show first signs by age 3 — trouble running, climbing stairs, or getting up from the floor.

Progressive muscle loss means most boys need a wheelchair by age 12. Leg muscles weaken first.

Key Fact

DMD is caused by mutations in the dystrophin gene — the largest gene in the human body with 79 exons.
